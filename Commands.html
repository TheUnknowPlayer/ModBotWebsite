Explore ModBot's commands.

ModBot uses application commands so the prefix is "/".

Commands:

/ban

Ban a member from your server.

/kick

Kick a member from your server.

/warn

Warn a member.

/mute

Silence anyone who speaks wrong.

/unmute

Unmute a member.

/lock

Lock a text channel.

/unlock

Unlock a locked channel.

/clear

Delete messages from a channel.
